# Prompt for Gemini Image Generation - À Propos Page

## Main Prompt:

Create a professional, modern hero image for the "À propos" (About Us) page of YL Solutions, a French company that connects clients with qualified construction professionals. 

**Visual Style:**
- Professional, trustworthy, and modern aesthetic
- Warm, inviting color palette with primary brand colors (consider blues, oranges, or warm neutrals)
- High quality, realistic photography style or professional illustration
- Suitable for use as a website hero image (landscape orientation, 16:9 or 21:9 aspect ratio)

**Key Elements to Include:**
- Construction or building industry context (but not too literal - more about connection and trust)
- Professional handshake or collaboration between people (representing the connection between clients and professionals)
- Modern construction elements in the background (subtle architectural lines, building silhouettes, or construction tools)
- Warm, welcoming atmosphere that conveys trust and professionalism
- Clean, uncluttered composition with space for text overlay

**Mood & Atmosphere:**
- Trustworthy and reliable
- Professional yet approachable
- Modern and innovative
- Focus on human connection and partnership
- Positive, forward-looking energy

**Technical Requirements:**
- Resolution: High resolution suitable for web (1920x1080 minimum, or 2560x1440)
- Format: JPG or PNG
- Style: Professional photography or high-quality digital illustration
- Color: Vibrant but professional, with good contrast for text overlay

**Avoid:**
- Overly busy or cluttered compositions
- Dark or gloomy tones
- Generic stock photo look
- Too literal construction site imagery (should be more refined)

---

## Alternative Shorter Prompt:

"Professional hero image for a construction services company's About Us page. Professional photography style, warm color palette, clean composition with space for text overlay. Conveys trust, professionalism, and human connection in the construction industry. High resolution, landscape orientation."
